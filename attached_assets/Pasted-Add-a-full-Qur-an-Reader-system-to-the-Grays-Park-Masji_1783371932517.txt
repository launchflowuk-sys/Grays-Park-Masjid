- Preferred source: Quran.com / Quran Foundation API.
- Fallback source: AlQuran.cloud API.

- Light mode
- Dark mode

- Play full surah

- Reciter dropdown

6. Qur’an Search

  - Surah name
  - Ayah number

- Bookmark any ayah

9. Share / Copy

10. Mobile Experience
