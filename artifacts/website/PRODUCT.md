# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Local Muslim community members (all ages) and newcomers/visitors to Grays Park Masjid, checking prayer times, services, events, and how to get involved or donate — often on mobile, sometimes glancing quickly (e.g. checking the next prayer time before leaving the house).

## Product Purpose
A welcoming, informative home for the masjid online: accurate prayer times, community services and education, events/announcements, a donation channel for the new mosque building project, and a gallery showing real community life. Success looks like visitors trusting the site as the authoritative source for the masjid and feeling the same warmth and sanctity they would walking into the building.

## Brand Personality
Elegant, traditional, dignified — rooted in classic Islamic architecture and ornament, but calm rather than ornate. Islamic geometric patterns and arch motifs are a deliberate part of the visual language, used as accents, not wallpaper.

## Anti-references
Must not look like generic AI-generated or templated SaaS/startup design: no stock hero-metric blocks, no gradient text, no eyebrow-labeled section scaffolding, no cookie-cutter icon+heading+text card grids, no glassmorphism-as-decoration. This is a sacred project — it should feel intentional, crafted, and specific to this masjid, not assembled from a generic component kit.

## Design Principles
- Dignity over decoration: ornament (arches, geometric patterns, Islamic stars) earns its place; never applied uniformly as scaffolding.
- Calm authority: the site is a trusted reference (prayer times, events) first, a persuasive brand surface second.
- Real community, real place: prioritize actual photos (gallery, events, the mosque building) over illustration or stock imagery.
- Warm but not casual: welcoming tone without losing the formality appropriate to a place of worship.
- Legible at a glance: prayer times and key info must read instantly, even for a quick mobile check.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Body text must meet WCAG AA contrast (≥4.5:1) against backgrounds, including on any patterned or tinted surfaces. Design for a broad age range (older community members are frequent users) — no reliance on hover-only interactions, comfortable tap targets, and respect `prefers-reduced-motion`.
